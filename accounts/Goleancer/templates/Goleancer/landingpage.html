Have a question? Check out the FAQ

We will answer your questions.

How can I choose the right freelancer for my project?

You can choose a freelancer based on their skills, experience, and reviews from previous projects. On this platform, each freelancer has a profile with a portfolio, ratings, and feedback from previous clients, which will help you assess the quality of their work. You can also chat with the freelancer directly to ensure they understand your project needs.

What should I do if I'm not satisfied with the freelancer's work?

If you're not satisfied with the work, you can contact the freelancer and provide feedback or request revisions according to the agreement. If the issue persists, you can contact customer support to resolve the issue or report your dissatisfaction.

Can I request revisions from the freelancer if the work does not meet my expectations?

Yes, most freelancers on this platform offer revisions as part of the agreement. Make sure to agree on the number of revisions included before starting the project. If you need more revisions than agreed upon, you can discuss this directly with the freelancer.

How long does it usually take to complete a project?

The time to complete a project depends on the type and complexity of the work. The freelancer will give an estimated timeline when they bid on the project. If there are any changes to the timeline, the freelancer will notify you proactively.

Can I ask a freelancer to start working on the project directly, or do I have to wait for a proposal?

You can invite a freelancer directly to work on your project. If the freelancer accepts the invitation, the project will start immediately. Alternatively, you can post the project and wait for freelancers to submit proposals.

Can I negotiate the price with the freelancer?

Yes, some freelancers are open to negotiating the price depending on the scope of work and the complexity of the project. You can discuss the budget directly with the freelancer to come to a mutually agreed-upon rate. However, please note that certain projects may have fixed prices set by the freelancer.
